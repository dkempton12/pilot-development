The Pilot Team

Eden Milroy, President

Mr. Milroy's career has included commercial, industrial, institutional and residential development. His long background as developer, investor and architect has given him a deep understanding of the many factors underlying today's building and investment decisions and realities. He is a member of the American Institute of Architects and a LEED AP.

Management - Planning - Owner’s Services - Architecture - Energy

Fred Kempton, Real Estate Consulting

Mr. Kempton conducts property valuation and provides real estate consulting services to office and industrial clients within the Greater Boston area. He identifies well located “added-value” multi-family, R&D, office and industrial properties for our investment clients.

Acquisitions analysis - Asset management - Highest and best use

Chuenfung Lin, AIA, VP Development Planning

With responsibility for projects from $5 million to $400 million, C.F. Lin’s experience encompasses project design, AE team management, consultant coordination, relations with user groups, clients and government agencies, and facilitating permitting for public agencies. His projects have included laboratory, campus and planning projects, office buildings, hotels, housing, historic preservation, institutional and government. He and Eden Milroy have collaborated on high-rise projects overseas. Mr. Lin brings important large project planning capabilities to Pilot.

Kathryn Maynes, Vice President

As residential property manager for several ownership groups in more than a dozen buildings, she has overseen day-to-day portfolio operations, including supervision of staff and coordination of contractors and vendors, collections and accounting. With Pilot since 2002, she has supported construction administration as well as managing commercial and industrial properties, including remote monitoring of security and building infrastructure.

Capital Programs Administration - Property Management
